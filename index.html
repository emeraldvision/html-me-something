Home Comforts:The Art and Science of Keeping House
Cheryl Mendelson

Mendelson's book is the result of her research into a topic that she recognized was not represented in modern literature. Missing the housekeeping books from generations past, Mendelson created this tome to address domestic tasks in an era of dishwashers, computers, and other technology.
